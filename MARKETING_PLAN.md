# GeoQB Marketing Plan

**Building the Future of Spatial Knowledge Graphs**

---

## Executive Summary

GeoQB is positioned to revolutionize geospatial analysis by combining graph databases, machine learning, and open data in a developer-friendly platform. This marketing plan outlines our strategy to capture the $15B+ geospatial intelligence market through product-led growth, content marketing, and strategic partnerships.

**Target:** $6.6M ARR by Year 3
**Primary Market:** Real Estate Tech, Urban Planning, ESG Analytics
**Key Differentiator:** Graph-based multi-layer analysis with data-as-code methodology

## Market Positioning

### Positioning Statement

**For** data scientists, urban planners, and sustainability analysts
**Who** need to integrate and analyze multi-source geospatial data
**GeoQB** is a spatial knowledge graph platform
**That** enables reproducible, graph-based analysis with a data-as-code approach
**Unlike** traditional GIS tools that are expensive and complex
**We** make geospatial graph analysis accessible, affordable, and collaborative

### Key Messages by Audience

#### Data Scientists & Developers
> "Build spatial knowledge graphs as easily as managing infrastructure with Terraform. Version control your geospatial analyses, collaborate with Git, and leverage graph ML out of the box."

**Key Points:**
- Data-as-code methodology
- Graph ML built-in (Node2Vec, clustering)
- Python-native, Jupyter-friendly
- Open source core

#### Urban Planners & Researchers
> "Understand your city through multi-layer graph analysis. Combine infrastructure, demographics, and services data to identify patterns and inform better decisions."

**Key Points:**
- Multi-source data integration
- Visual analysis tools
- Reproducible workflows
- Cost-effective alternative to ArcGIS

#### ESG & Sustainability Professionals
> "Automatically score location sustainability using open data. Generate transparent, auditable ESG reports that stakeholders trust."

**Key Points:**
- Automated sustainability scoring
- Multi-factor analysis
- Transparent methodology
- Export-ready reports

#### Real Estate & PropTech
> "Transform property analysis with AI-powered location intelligence. Score neighborhoods on sustainability, accessibility, and livability in seconds."

**Key Points:**
- Instant sustainability scores
- Neighborhood analytics
- Competitive intelligence
- API integration

## Campaign Ideas

### 1. "30 Days of Geospatial Graphs"

**Format:** Daily tutorials in November (Geographic Information Systems Day month)

**Content:**
- Day 1-10: Basics (setup, first layer, queries)
- Day 11-20: Intermediate (multi-layer, ML, streaming)
- Day 21-30: Advanced (custom integrations, production deployment)

**Promotion:**
- Twitter/LinkedIn daily posts
- YouTube tutorials
- Blog post summaries
- GitHub repo with all examples
- Certificate for completion

**Goal:** 1,000 participants, 200 conversions to paid

### 2. "Sustainability Score Challenge"

**Format:** Community competition

**Challenge:** Build the best sustainability scoring model using GeoQB

**Prizes:**
- 1st: $5,000 + 1 year Business plan free
- 2nd: $2,000 + 6 months Professional free
- 3rd: $1,000 + 3 months Professional free

**Judging:**
- Innovation (40%)
- Data sources used (30%)
- Documentation (20%)
- Visual presentation (10%)

**Timeline:** 6 weeks (2 weeks submissions, 2 weeks judging, 2 weeks promotion)

**Goal:** 50 submissions, press coverage, community growth

**Format:** 4-week online course

**Curriculum:**
- Week 1: Graph fundamentals & GeoQB basics

- Week 3: Graph machine learning

**Delivery:**
- Live weekly sessions (1 hour)

- Certificate on completion

**Pricing:**
- Free for Community tier users
- Included for Professional+ users
- $99 for others

**Goal:** 200 enrollees, 60% completion, 20% upgrade to paid

### 4. "Open Data Week"
